Dear President Obama, Take a stand for Net Neutrality

110,000 out of 200,000 signatures

Dear President Obama,

Millions of us commented in the FCC rulemaking process. Now we need you to back us up.

We, the users of the Internet, petition you to issue a statement expressing your strong support for reclassifying broadband as a telecommunications service under Title II of the Communications Act, rather than the sender side proposals the FCC is considering.

The White House’s voice is a strong one. Your support for Title II reclassification of broadband service will be deeply influential.

Anything less than full Title II reclassification – combined, as it must be, with forbearance from regulation beyond specific and narrow “rules of the road” – will be confusing and risky at best. We need clear rules that will survive a legal challenge.
